Help & Usage

Use this page to get quick guidance for scanning and safely shutting down devices in your authorized network.

Scanning

Enter a CIDR network like 192.168.1.0/24 and click Start Scan. The tool will discover active hosts and show them in the results table.

Exporting results

After scanning, use the Download CSV button to save device data for reporting or review.

Remote shutdown

Select a device IP from the dropdown, choose the target OS, and send the shutdown command. Linux/macOS devices require SSH username and private key path.

Safety tips

  • Only scan networks and devices you own or are explicitly authorized to manage.
  • Do not issue shutdown commands to mobile or IoT devices.
  • Use admin credentials responsibly for Windows RPC shutdown and SSH shutdown.
